About Us

We, Axon Active Vietnam, are a member of Axon Active Group, leading Swiss IT Corporation. Our first move began in 2008 as the Group expanded its business to Vietnam with vision to establish a high-performance, scalable software delivery infrastructure. We are pioneer in applying Agile/Scrum to software development in Vietnam. We offer our international clients high quality, professional, flexible and affordable offshore development solutions:

  • Offshore Development Center (ODC)
  • Offshore Testing Center (OTC)
  • Agile Coaching and ICT Services

Axon Active is now house to 450+ developers with diversified mix of skillsets, technology and business domains. Our profound practice in Agile methodologies and core values help us build up trust and various successful projects for small-to-large corporate clients worldwide.
